About Professor David Ryan

Professor David Ryan is a dedicated educator whose teaching combines clarity, compassion, creativity, and deep expertise. His Emergent Media & Technology course has transformed students into responsible, reflective, and ethically minded communicators prepared for real-world media environments.

Teaching Philosophy

Professor Ryan creates a warm, inclusive, and intellectually rich environment. Through sprints, discussions, interactive activities, and structured learning, he helps students understand communication, emerging media, and professional practice in meaningful ways.

Impact on Students

As a student in his class, I learned how to grow as a communicator, researcher, and ethical professional. His support strengthened my academic foundation, prepared me for advanced research and doctoral studies, and helped shape my journey as a digital storyteller.

“Professor Ryan is a life-builder. His mentorship does not just improve assignments — it shapes confidence, clarity, and future success.”
